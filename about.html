At Lumari Films, we believe every actor has a story worth telling—and we’re here to help you tell it with cinematic impact.

We specialize in professional demo reel production that goes beyond just filming scenes. Our process covers every stage of filmmaking—from script development and creative direction to cinematography, editing, and color grading—all executed with industry-standard equipment and a deep understanding of cinematic storytelling.

What sets us apart is our personalized approach. We don’t believe in one-size-fits-all reels. We take the time to understand you, your strengths, and your goals, crafting original scenes that showcase your talent in the most authentic and compelling way possible. Whether you’re building your first reel or elevating your portfolio, we’re committed to creating work that gets you noticed.

With hands-on expertise in every phase of production, Lumari Films is your creative partner in bringing your best performances to life—on screen and on point.

Create Original Footage That Elevates Your Demo Reel

We provide fully customized production services designed to deliver cinematic-quality demo reels tailored specifically to your acting style and career goals.

Beyond just shooting a scene, you’ll gain valuable on-set experience, deepen your understanding of the production process, and receive expert guidance from seasoned filmmakers.

Pre-Production

Our initial consultation is designed to get to know you—your background, your experience, and where you want to go in your acting career. Together, we explore your personal brand and typecast, brainstorm creative concepts, and identify roles that genuinely excite you. From there, we’ll collaborate on the script, and lock in a production date to bring your vision to life.

Production

Production day is an exciting milestone. Arrive rested, prepared, and ready to perform—this is your moment. While you focus on rehearsing and delivering your best on camera, our crew takes care of everything behind the scenes, from lighting to technical setup. Lights, camera, action—we’ll handle the rest.

Post-Production

Welcome to the final stage—where your footage is shaped into a cinematic scene. We carefully edit your best performances, crafting a polished result that feels straight out of a film or TV show. Your feedback is important to us, and we make sure you’re fully satisfied with the final cut.
